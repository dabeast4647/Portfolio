Who am I?

Born in 2005, Kandy, Sri Lanka. I believe that web design can be more diverse and inspiring. Throughout my journey as a freelance developer, I've worked for many companies, collaborated with talented individuals to create open source projects and consumer products. I'm naturally curious and I enjoy debugging problems :)

My Services

Web Design

For the past 3 years, I've been forming digital products the likes of interactive experiences, corporate websites, e-commerce or mobile apps for companies all in Europe and North America. From research, anti-virus, rendering, wireframes to final design systems, user interfaces, motion design and brand guidelines.

Software

Between March 2021 and January 2022, I took up an internship at secure-soft. A company specializing in anti-virus software, my position was to develop and maintain the whitelist system for our customers. Aside from this I also have some open source projects I contributed towards which are avalible on my github profile.

Programming

Since the time of developing my previous portfolio, my abilities have significantly changed. I am now much more capable in Web-development (CSS/HTML/JS), and my C# knowledge has grown exponentially although, at the moment I can't see any practical uses for it.

Visuals

One goal I had was to develop my visual skills regarding user-interaces, rendering and video editing. A goal which I can happily say I succesfully completed over the course of the last few months. After many hours of guides, tutorials and books. I have managed to develop my Web-Design and Rendering abilities.
